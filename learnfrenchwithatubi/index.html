Bonjour!
Growing up in Nigeria, my exposure to languages was primarily confined to my native tongue. However, an insatiable curiosity for new linguistic horizons ignited within me. This fervor propelled me on a transformative odyssey to the Democratic Republic of Congo, where I resided for two pivotal years. It was there that my fascination with the French language and the rich tapestry of its accompanying culture truly took root. Returning to Nigeria, I was stirred by an impassioned resolve to narrow the divide between communities and the French language. I harbored an earnest desire to elucidate the intricacies of French cultures and lifestyles. I firmly advocate that genuine language acquisition burgeons from immersive experiences, where one is fully steeped in both the linguistic nuances and the cultural ethos. For me, language serves not just as a vessel for culture but as an inseparable facet of it. Moreover, my unwavering belief endorses the idea that mastery of a language demands the courage to venture beyond one's comfort zone, particularly during the nascent stages of learning.

Cultivating confidence becomes indispensable in the pursuit of linguistic proficiency, empowering one to navigate the challenges of acquiring a new language. This transformative journey has solidified my belief in the profound connection between language and culture, instilling in me a fervent dedication to foster cross-cultural understanding through the conduit of language acquisition. Currently, I run Learn French with Atubi, where we offer French lessons online and on-site in Nigeria. Through Learn French with Atubi, you can be certain of learning REAL French as it is spoken in France while building your confidence. We are all Experienced Teachers of French, extremely friendly and always happy to teach you. We are here to take you on a Fantastic French adventure and You will love it! À bientôt
